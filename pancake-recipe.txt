#new recipe for Pancakes

- 1 Cup of Flour
- 2 Spoons of Sugar
- 1/4 cup of Milk
- 1 Spoon of butter
- 1 small spoon of Vanilla
- 1 egg


Preparation:

 Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl and
 heat the pan around 3 minutes (medium heat) then
 put the mix into the pan and wait until start to pin some bubbles
 then turn around another 3 minutes and serve with honey and butter.
